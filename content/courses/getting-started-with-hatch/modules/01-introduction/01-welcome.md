# Welcome to Hatch

Hatch is a platform for creating and delivering beautiful online courses. Whether you're teaching programming, design, cooking, or anything else — Hatch gives you the tools to share your knowledge with the world.

## What You'll Learn

In this course, you'll discover how to:

- Structure your course into modules and lessons
- Write content using Markdown
- Add images and videos to your lessons
- Use the teleprompter to record your course
- Publish and share your course with learners

## Why Hatch?

Hatch was built with creators in mind. We believe that great courses start with great content, and great content starts with simple, powerful tools.

Let's get started.
